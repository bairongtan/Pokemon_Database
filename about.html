About Page

This is a database that showcases various dataclasses that have been leaked from website data breaches throughout several years. These dataclasses include: email addresses, passwords, phone numbers, IP addresses, names, and physical addresses.
Steps to use this database:
1. Click on the "Load Data" button, a pie chart will appear showing the total amount of each dataclass is in the database.
2. Click on the drop-down menu to select a year to filter all the data.
3. Click on the "Filter Data" button to generate a pie chart consisting of all dataclasses from the selected year.
4. To clear out all of the data, click on the "Clear Data" button, this will reload the browser and delete the current pie chart as well as the locally stored data.
